  • ➤ {%trans "Where do I access past papers?" %}

    {%trans "Past examination papers can be accessed via the Library Portal" %}

  • ➤ {%trans "How can I access my exam timetable?" %}

    {%trans "Timetables are available on the TU Dublin website exam section and are published approximately one month before exams commence. Publish dates will be widely advertised through social media and email." %}

{%trans "Location" %}

  • ➤{%trans " How can I find the location of my exams?" %}

    {%trans "The location of the exam will be detailed to each student in advance of the date normally with the exam timetable. Students should familiarize themselves with the location of the exam prior to the date of their first exam. On occasion the location of an exam may change for operational reasons, students should check the website for updates in advance of the day." %}

{%trans "Examination" %}

  • ➤{%trans "Do I need to register for exams?" %}

    {%trans "All registered students are automatically entered for exams." %}

  • ➤ {%trans "What do I need to gain entry to the exam venue on the day?" %}

    {%trans "All students should present a current Student ID Card to gain entry to an examination venue. Alternative photographic ID is also acceptable i.e. Passport or Garda ID." %}

  • ➤ {%trans "What happens if I am late for the exam?" %}

    {%trans "Normally, no student shall be admitted to the Exam more than one half hour after the start of the exam; in exceptional circumstances however, and provided that no other student sitting the same paper has left the Exam a student may be admitted later, at the discretion of the Exam Secretary. In such circumstances extra time will not normally be allowed." %}

  • ➤ {%trans "What happens if I think I may have the wrong exam paper?" %}

    {%trans "If you think you have the wrong exam paper it is important that you notify and check with the exam invigilator immediately, it is up to you to ensure you are in possession of the correct paper and that you follow the instructions printed on the exam paper and the answer book." %}

  • ➤ {%trans "Do I need to confirm my attendance at the exam?" %}

    {%trans "Students shall sign the Attendance Form when requested by the Invigilator, and shall leave their Student ID Card in a visible position on the desk." %}

{%trans "Special Arrangements, Illness and withdrawal" %}

  • ➤ {%trans "I am a student with a disability , I need extra help during the exams, what do I do?" %}

    {%trans "If you are a student with a disability, ensure you register with the Disability Service well in advance of the exam to ensure special arrangements can be put in place for the duration of exams." %}

  • ➤{%trans "I need to take medication during my exam, should I tell someone before the exam starts?" %}

    {%trans "Students required to take medication during an exam must inform the invigilator prior to commencement of the exam." %}

  • ➤ {%trans "What happens if there is a serious incident during the exam?" %}

    {%trans "In the event of fire or other serious cause the safety of the students and the Institutes staff shall be the primary concern and the Invigilator(s) shall evacuate the Exam in an orderly manner and without delay. In such circumstances the exam shall be declared void and shall be rescheduled." %}
